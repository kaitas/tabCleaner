Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024-01-12

1. Overview

"Tab Cleanup" (hereinafter referred to as "the Extension") respects your privacy. We operate under a "Local First" philosophy.

2. Data Collection

We do NOT collect your browsing history, personal information, or specific URLs on our servers without your explicit consent.

2.1 Local Data

All detailed tab data is stored locally in your browser (`chrome.storage.local`) or in the Google Spreadsheet you own and connected.

2.2 Statistical Data (Optional)

Only if you explicitly opt-in to the "Global Stats" feature, we collect anonymized data:

  • Randomly generated User ID (UUID)
  • Number of tabs closed
  • Timestamps

3. Third-Party Services

We use Cloudflare R2 and Workers for statistical data processing (if opted in).

4. Contact

Please contact us via GitHub Issues for any privacy concerns.
